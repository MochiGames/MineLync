To play on MineLync Minecraft server, launch Bedrock or Java Edition of Minecraft, and type in the IP of play.minelync.com or mc.minelync.com.
If you are playing on Java, that's it! For Bedrock, you'll need to add the port: 25565. Now you can play on Bedrock!
